Quick Tips

Small reminders while editing.

  • Paint placements: Click or drag across cells to paint the selected placement
  • Auto-numbering: TOP/BTM auto-show as TOP2/BTM2 when multiple appear in a column
  • Auto-fill OUT: After ELIM/EXTM, later empty cells become "Out" automatically
  • Tie ranks: Multiple eliminations or LSFTC losers share the same rank automatically
  • Reorder: Drag contestants/episodes via the ⋮⋮ handle, or drag from the table's left gutter
  • Photos: Click a photo placeholder → Upload or Paste from clipboard
  • Undo/Redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y works for placements, edits, and photo changes
  • Borders & Subtexts: Use separate palette tabs to add border overlays or mini-challenge labels
  • Export: Copy HTML or save as PNG for wiki/website use
